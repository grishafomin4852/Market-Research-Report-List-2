<p><p>The Electric Slip Ring market includes two types, namely Enclosed Power Thru-Bore and Enclosed Signal Thru-Bore. Enclosed Power Thru-Bore slip rings offer the transmission of both power and signals simultaneously. They are designed with a central bore allowing the passage of power and signal conductors. On the other hand, Enclosed Signal Thru-Bore slip rings are specifically used for signal transmission, with no capability to transmit power. They also feature a central bore for signal conductors, enabling the uninterrupted transfer of signals in various applications.</p></p>
