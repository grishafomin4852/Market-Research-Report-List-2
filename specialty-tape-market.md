<p><p>Specialty tapes are classified into various types based on their material composition and specific application requirements. PVC tapes are made of polyvinyl chloride and provide excellent electrical insulation and moisture resistance. Paper tapes, on the other hand, are biodegradable and commonly used in painting and packaging applications. PP tapes are made of polypropylene and are known for their high tensile strength and resistance to chemicals. Foam tapes have a cushion-like structure and are used for sealing, mounting, and dampening applications. PET tapes, made of polyethylene terephthalate, offer high temperature resistance and are commonly used in automotive and electronics industries. Lastly, "Others" encompass a range of specialty tapes made from different materials such as cloth, metal, or acrylic, catering to specific applications like medical, construction, or aerospace industries.</p></p>
