<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>In the mobile device market, there are four main types of touch screens: capacitive, resistive, surface acoustic wave, and infrared. Capacitive touch screens use the electrical properties of the human body to detect touch, providing high responsiveness and multi-touch capabilities. Resistive touch screens use layers of conductive and resistive materials, requiring pressure to register touch. Surface acoustic wave touch screens use ultrasonic waves that are disrupted by touch, delivering excellent image clarity. Infrared touch screens use an array of infrared sensors to detect touch, offering high durability and accuracy.</p></p>
