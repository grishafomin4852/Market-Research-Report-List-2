<p><p>Corn starch is available in two main types: Non-GM Corn Starch and General Corn Starch. Non-GM Corn Starch is derived from non-genetically modified corn, ensuring that the product is free from any genetic modifications. This type of corn starch is preferred by consumers who are concerned about the potential health and environmental risks associated with genetically modified organisms. On the other hand, General Corn Starch refers to the commonly available corn starch in the market, which may or may not be derived from genetically modified corn.</p></p>
