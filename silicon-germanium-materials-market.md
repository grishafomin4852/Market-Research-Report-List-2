<p><p>The Silicon Germanium Materials Market can be divided into three main types: source materials, substrate materials, and epitaxial wafers. Source materials refer to the raw components used to produce silicon germanium alloys. Substrate materials are the base upon which the epitaxial layer is grown, providing structural support. Epitaxial wafers are the final products that consist of a thin layer of single-crystal silicon germanium. These types work together in the production process, with source materials being transformed into epitaxial wafers through the use of substrate materials.</p></p>
