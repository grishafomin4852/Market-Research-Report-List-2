<p><p>The high pass electronic filter market includes two main types: passive electronic filters and active electronic filters. </p><p>Passive electronic filters are simple circuits that use only passive components such as resistors, capacitors, and inductors to attenuate frequencies below a certain cut-off point. They are cost-effective and widely used in various applications.</p><p>On the other hand, active electronic filters, which require a power supply, employ active components such as operational amplifiers to amplify and process signals. They offer greater flexibility and accuracy compared to passive filters but are generally more expensive.</p></p>
