<p><p>The composition resistor market is segmented into three types: carbon-composition resistors, carbon-film resistors, and metal-film resistors. Carbon-composition resistors are made of a mixture of carbon and an insulating material, offering stability and high precision. Carbon-film resistors have a thin layer of carbon deposited on a ceramic substrate, providing low temperature coefficient and low noise. Metal-film resistors are made by depositing a thin film of metal onto a substrate, offering high stability, accuracy, and lower noise compared to other types. Each type caters to specific applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
