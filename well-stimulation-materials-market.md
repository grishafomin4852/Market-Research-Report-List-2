<p><p>The well stimulation materials market consists of various types of materials used in the oil and gas industry to enhance the productivity of wells. Proppants are solid materials like sand or ceramic beads that are injected to keep fractures open and facilitate the flow of hydrocarbons. Base fluid materials are used as carriers for proppants during hydraulic fracturing. Polymer gelling agents are additives that increase the viscosity of fluids, improving their ability to carry proppants. Biocides and surfactants are chemicals used to control microbial growth and enhance fluid performance during well stimulation.</p></p>
