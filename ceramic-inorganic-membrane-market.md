<p><p>The Ceramic Inorganic Membrane Market consists of two main types: flat-sheet membrane and pipe membrane. Flat-sheet membranes are thin, flat structures that are used for applications such as wastewater treatment, gas separation, and nanofiltration. They are typically made from ceramic materials and offer high permeability and selectivity. On the other hand, pipe membranes are cylindrical in shape and are commonly used for liquid filtration and purification. They provide excellent chemical resistance and can withstand high pressure and temperature conditions. Both types of membranes play a crucial role in various industrial processes.</p></p>
