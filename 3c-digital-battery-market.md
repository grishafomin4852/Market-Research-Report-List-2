<p><p>The 3C digital battery market encompasses two main types: cylindrical batteries and prismatic batteries. Cylindrical batteries are often used in portable electronic devices and have a long cylindrical shape, with a positive and negative electrode placed inside a cylindrical shell. These batteries are known for their high energy density and durability. On the other hand, prismatic batteries have a flat, rectangular shape, offering flexibility in design and space utilization. They are frequently used in thin devices like smartphones and tablets, providing efficient power solutions.</p></p>
