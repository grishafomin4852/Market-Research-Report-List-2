<p><p>The multimode optical transceiver market is categorized into various types. CFP, CFP2, and CFP4 are larger transceivers used for high-speed applications. XFP refers to 10-gigabit small form-factor pluggable transceivers, while CXP is used for high-density applications.</p></p>
