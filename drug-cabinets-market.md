<p><p>Drug cabinets are storage units designed to securely store medications and drugs in various healthcare settings. There are four main types of drug cabinets available in the market. Tall drug cabinets are floor-standing units that provide maximum storage capacity. Wall drug cabinets are mounted on walls, saving space and ensuring easy access to medications. Base drug cabinets are placed on the floor but provide lower storage capacity compared to tall cabinets. Bench-mounted drug cabinets are portable and can be placed on countertops, offering convenient access to medications in smaller healthcare settings.</p></p>
