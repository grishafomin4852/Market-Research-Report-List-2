<p><p>Electrical steels are a type of specialty steel used in the production of electrical components. Oriented electrical steels have a unique grain structure that allows them to be magnetized in a specific direction, making them suitable for use in transformers and motors. Non-oriented electrical steels, on the other hand, have a random grain structure that makes them ideal for applications where magnetic flux is constantly changing direction, such as in electric generators. Both types of electrical steels play a crucial role in the efficient functioning of electrical equipment.</p></p>
