<p><p>The coil cleaner market is categorized into acid-based cleaners and non-acid cleaners. Acid-based cleaners are formulated with acidic compounds that effectively remove tough stains, dirt, and mineral deposits from coil surfaces. They are commonly used for heavily soiled coils but require proper safety precautions due to their corrosive nature. On the other hand, non-acid cleaners are gentle on surfaces and utilize alternative cleaning agents like detergents or enzyme-based solutions. They are preferred for regular maintenance and are safer to handle compared to acid-based cleaners.</p></p>
