Type-2 suits are similar to Type-1 but offer limited protection against liquid chemicals. Type-3 suits are liquid-tight but not gas-tight. Type-4 suits protect against splashes and sprays but do not provide full-body coverage. Type-5 suits protect against airborne solid particles, and Type-6 suits offer limited protection against liquid mist.</p></p>
