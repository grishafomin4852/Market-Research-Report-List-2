<p><p>The circuit protection kits market comprises different types of products, namely circuit breakers, electronic fuses, power thyristors, and others. Circuit breakers are devices designed to automatically interrupt the flow of electric current to protect the circuit from damage. Electronic fuses work similarly but use a solid-state device instead. Power thyristors are semiconductor devices used for controlling high power electric circuits. The "others" category encompasses various circuit protection products that do not fall into the aforementioned categories, such as surge protectors, voltage regulators, and lightning arresters.</p></p>
