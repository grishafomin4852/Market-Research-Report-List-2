<p><p>Flexible AMOLED Panels refer to display panels that use the Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) technology. This type of display offers high-resolution, vibrant colors, and wide viewing angles. It is commonly used in smartphones, tablets, and televisions. On the other hand, Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) is another type of OLED display technology that uses a simpler structure. PMOLED displays are less expensive but have lower resolution, slower response time, and limited color range. They are mainly used in smaller devices such as wearable gadgets and small appliances.</p></p>
