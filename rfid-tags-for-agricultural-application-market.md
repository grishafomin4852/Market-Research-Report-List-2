<p><strong>The RFID Tags for Agricultural Application Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID tags are used in agricultural applications to track and monitor livestock, crops, and equipment. There are two types of RFID tags in the market: active and passive. Active RFID tags have built-in power sources, such as batteries, and can transmit data over longer distances. They are typically more expensive and suitable for tracking large animals or vehicles. On the other hand, passive RFID tags do not have a power source and rely on the energy from the reader to transmit data. They are more cost-effective but have a shorter read range, making them suitable for tracking smaller items like crops or tools.</p></p>
