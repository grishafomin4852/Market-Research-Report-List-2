<p><p>Fine Metal Mask (FMM) is a technology used in the production of OLED displays. It involves three market types: etching, electroforming, and multi-material composite method. In the etching method, the metal mask is developed by selectively removing the unwanted portions. Electroforming method creates the mask by electrodepositing metal onto a mold. The third method, multi-material composite, involves combining different materials to form the mask. These market types enable the production of precise and high-resolution OLED displays, enhancing image quality and overall performance.</p></p>
