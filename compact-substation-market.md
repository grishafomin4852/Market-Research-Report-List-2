<p><p>Compact substations are used for transforming high voltage electricity to low voltage electricity for distribution. The market is categorized into two types: metal shell and non-metallic shell. Metal shell compact substations are made of durable materials like stainless steel or aluminium, which provide better protection against environmental factors like moisture and corrosion. Non-metallic shell compact substations are constructed using materials like fiber-reinforced composites or plastic, offering advantages such as lightweight construction and resistance to chemical corrosion. Both types of compact substations are designed to efficiently house and protect electrical equipment in a compact and secure manner.</p></p>
