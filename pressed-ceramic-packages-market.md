<p><p>Pressed Ceramic Packages Market Types refer to different types of packaging materials used in electronic components. Ceramic-metal sealing (CERTM) involves bonding ceramic and metal materials to create a hermetic seal. Glass-metal sealing (GTMS) is a similar process where glass and metal are bonded. Passivation glass is a type of glass used to protect electronic components from environmental factors. Transponder glass is used in RFID transponders. Reed glass is a type of glass used in reed switch packaging. These materials are crucial for ensuring the durability and functionality of electronic components.</p></p>
