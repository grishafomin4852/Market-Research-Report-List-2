<p><p>Utility agricultural adjuvants are substances added to agricultural chemicals to enhance their efficiency and effectiveness. Compatibility agents ensure the compatibility of different chemical components used in a mixture, while buffers/acidifiers adjust the pH levels for optimal performance. Antifoam agents prevent excessive foaming during application, and water conditioners improve the quality and penetration of water used in mixtures. Anti-drift agents minimize drift and maximize the coverage of products. Other utility adjuvants include dispersants, wetting agents, and spreaders, which improve product distribution and coverage. These adjuvants play a crucial role in maximizing the effectiveness of agricultural chemicals.</p></p>
