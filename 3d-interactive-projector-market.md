Each technology offers unique advantages, such as DLP's excellent color accuracy, LCD's vibrant colors, and LCoS's ability to produce high-resolution images. Customers can choose from these market types based on their specific requirements for 3D interactive projection, such as image quality, color reproduction, or resolution.</p></p>
