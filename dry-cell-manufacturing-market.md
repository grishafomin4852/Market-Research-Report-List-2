<p><p>The dry cell manufacturing market comprises two main types: carbon zinc manganese battery and alkaline zinc manganese battery. Carbon zinc manganese batteries are commonly used in low-drain devices such as remote controls and flashlights. They offer a lower capacity and shorter lifespan compared to alkaline batteries. Alkaline zinc manganese batteries, on the other hand, are suitable for high-drain devices like digital cameras and portable gaming consoles, as they provide longer life and higher capacity. Both types of batteries are widely used in various consumer electronic devices.</p></p>
