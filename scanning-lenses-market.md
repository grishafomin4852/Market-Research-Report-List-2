<p><p>The scanning lenses market can be categorized into different types, including telecentric type and others. Telecentric lenses are designed to ensure that the object being scanned remains in focus, irrespective of its distance from the lens, making them ideal for applications requiring accurate measurements. On the other hand, the "others" category includes various types of lenses such as non-telecentric lenses, fixed focal length lenses, zoom lenses, and wide-angle lenses. These lenses cater to different scanning requirements based on factors like field of view, resolution, and magnification, providing versatility for a range of scanning applications.</p></p>
