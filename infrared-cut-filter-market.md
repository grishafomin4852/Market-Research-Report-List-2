<p><strong>The Infrared Cut Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Infrared Cut Filter</li><li>Absorption Infrared Cut Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infrared Cut Filter market consists of two types: reflective infrared cut filters and absorption infrared cut filters. Reflective infrared cut filters are composed of multiple layers that reflect infrared light while allowing visible light to pass through, effectively cutting off infrared wavelengths. On the other hand, absorption infrared cut filters absorb infrared light through a special material, preventing it from interfering with visible light transmission. Both types are used in various industries, such as photography, surveillance, and automotive, to improve image quality and enhance vision capabilities.</p></p>
