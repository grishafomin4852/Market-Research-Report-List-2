<p><p>Perfumes are available in various types, each differing in their concentration of fragrance oils and overall scent duration. Eau de Parfum contains a high concentration of oils for a long-lasting fragrance. Eau de Toilette has a lower concentration, making it lighter and suitable for daily wear. Eau de Cologne is even lighter and often includes citrus scents. Eau Fraiche has the lowest oil concentration, providing a refreshing and subtle aroma. These different perfume market types cater to different preferences and occasions, allowing individuals to find their preferred fragrance intensity.</p></p>
