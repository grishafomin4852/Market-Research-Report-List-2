<p><p>The monocular camera market includes various types of cameras designed for specific purposes. Night vision cameras are equipped with advanced technologies that enable capturing clear images and videos in low-light or dark environments. These cameras use infrared illuminators to enhance visibility in darkness. On the other hand, thermal imaging cameras detect heat signatures emitted by objects and create visible images based on the temperature differences. They are commonly used in security systems, firefighting, and industrial inspections, as they can identify anomalies or areas of concern based on temperature variations.</p></p>
