<p><p>The Leaf-Spine fabric switches market is comprised of two types of switches: Leaf switches and Spine switches. Leaf switches, also known as access switches, are responsible for connecting end-user devices, servers, and storage systems to the network fabric. They provide low-latency connectivity and high network throughput for efficient data transmission. On the other hand, Spine switches serve as the backbone of the network, connecting all the Leaf switches together. They ensure high-bandwidth and low-latency communication among Leaf switches, enabling scalable and flexible network architectures.</p></p>
