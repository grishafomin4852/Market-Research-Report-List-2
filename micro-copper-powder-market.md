<p><p>The Micro Copper Powder market can be classified into three categories based on the particle size: <300nm, 300-500nm, and >500nm. Copper powder with a size smaller than 300nm is considered to have the highest surface area, which enables better reactivity in various applications such as conductive inks, lubricants, and electronic components. Copper powder in the size range of 300-500nm offers a balance between increased surface area and ease of handling, making it suitable for uses like catalysts and sintering. Copper powder larger than 500nm is mainly utilized in manufacturing applications like brazing and welding due to its comparatively lower surface area.</p></p>
