<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Swept Frequency Capacitive Sensing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Capacitance</li><li>Projected Capacitance</li><li>Self-Capacitance</li><li>Mutual Capacitance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The swept frequency capacitive sensing market consists of four types: surface capacitance, projected capacitance, self-capacitance, and mutual capacitance. Surface capacitance involves sensing changes in capacitance caused by a user's touch on a conductive surface. Projected capacitance detects touch on a non-conductive surface using a grid of electrodes. Self-capacitance measures changes in capacitance when a single touch is detected on a sensor. Mutual capacitance also uses a grid of electrodes but detects multiple touches simultaneously. These technologies find applications in touchscreens, touchpads, and proximity sensors.</p></p>
