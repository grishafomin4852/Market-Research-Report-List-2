<p><p>The mini camera market includes different types of cameras with specific functionalities. One such type is the NIR Camera, which stands for Near-Infrared Camera. These cameras are designed to capture images beyond what is visible to the human eye, using wavelengths of light that are just outside the red end of the spectrum. They are commonly used in security systems, agriculture, and industrial applications. Similarly, the SWIR Camera, or Short-Wave Infrared Camera, operates in a slightly longer wavelength than NIR cameras. SWIR cameras are often used in applications such as surveillance, quality control, and semiconductor inspection.</p></p>
