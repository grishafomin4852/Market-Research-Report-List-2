<p><p>The automatic washing machine market primarily consists of two types: top-loading washing machines and agitator washing machines. Top-loading washing machines are designed with a lid on the top, allowing users to load and unload clothes from the machine's top. On the other hand, agitator washing machines contain a central post that extends from the bottom of the tub, which moves clothes around to clean them. Both types of machines offer convenient automatic washing features, providing ease and efficiency to users.</p></p>
