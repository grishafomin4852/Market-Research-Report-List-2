<p><p>Range Hoods Cooktops are devices that are installed above the cooktop or stove in the kitchen to exhaust smoke, odor, and grease that are produced during cooking. They are also known as exhaust hoods, kitchen hoods, or vent hoods. Range hoods have a fan or blower that helps in pulling the air and fumes out of the kitchen and expelling it outside the house. They can be ducted, meaning the air is exhausted outdoors through a duct system, or non-ducted, meaning the air is filtered and recirculated back into the kitchen.</p><p>The Range Hoods Cooktop Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing importance of maintaining indoor air quality and the rising awareness about the health risks associated with exposure to airborne pollutants are driving the demand for efficient range hoods. Additionally, the growing trend of open-concept kitchens and the increasing focus on kitchen aesthetics are also fueling the demand for stylish and visually appealing range hoods.</p><p>Market analysis suggests that the demand for range hoods is likely to increase due to the rising number of residential and commercial construction projects worldwide. Manufacturers are focusing on developing smart range hoods integrated with features like touch controls, LED lighting, and sensors for automatically adjusting fan speeds.
