<p><p>Omnidirectional antenna market types include monopole antenna, dipole antenna, and others. Monopole antennas are vertical poles that radiate signals in all directions, commonly used in radio and TV broadcasting. Dipole antennas, on the other hand, consist of two perpendicular conductive elements and are widely used in wireless communication systems. Other types of omnidirectional antennas include loop antennas, helical antennas, and collinear antennas. These antennas are designed to radiate signals uniformly in all directions and find applications in various industries such as telecommunications, aviation, and maritime.</p></p>
