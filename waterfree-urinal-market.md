<p><p>The waterfree urinal market consists of three main types: microbiological systems, liquid barrier systems, and valve barrier systems. Microbiological systems use natural bacteria to break down urine and eliminate odors. Liquid barrier systems use a lighter-than-water sealant, such as oil, to trap urine and prevent odors from escaping. Valve barrier systems have a self-closing valve that seals the drainpipe, preventing any odor from going back into the restroom. These different systems aim to provide an eco-friendly and cost-effective solution for water conservation in urinals.</p></p>
