<p><p>The traffic sensor market consists of various types of sensors utilized for monitoring and managing traffic flow. Inductive loop sensors are the most common type, comprising of wire loops embedded in the road that detect vehicles. Piezoelectric sensors convert pressure into electrical signals and are often used in parking lots. Bending plate sensors use flexible plates to measure vehicle weight. Image sensors employ cameras for vehicle detection. Infrared sensors use infrared light to detect the presence of vehicles. The market also includes other types of sensors like radar sensors and ultrasonic sensors for traffic management purposes.</p></p>
