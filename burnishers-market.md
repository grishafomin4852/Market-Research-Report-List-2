Walk-behind burnishers are manually operated machines for small to medium-sized areas. Sit-on burnishers are larger and are operated by a person sitting on it. Autonomous burnishers are self-driving machines that can operate without human intervention. Each type caters to different floor sizes and user preferences, providing efficient and convenient solutions for various cleaning needs.</p></p>
