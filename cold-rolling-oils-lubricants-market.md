<p><p>Cold rolling oils and lubricants are used in the metalworking industry to reduce friction and heat during the cold rolling process. There are three main types of cold rolling oils/lubricants: synthetic, mineral-based, and semi-synthetic. Synthetic cold rolling oils/lubricants are chemically formulated to provide excellent lubrication and cooling properties. Mineral-based oils/lubricants are derived from crude oil and offer good lubrication but may require more frequent replacement. Semi-synthetic oils/lubricants are a combination of synthetic and mineral-based components, providing a balance between lubrication effectiveness and cost. Each type offers different advantages and suitability for specific cold rolling applications.</p></p>
