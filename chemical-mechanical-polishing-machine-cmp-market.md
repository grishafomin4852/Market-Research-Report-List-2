CMP machines play a crucial role in achieving the required specifications for these devices.</p><p>Additionally, the rising investment in research and development activities for semiconductor manufacturing and related technologies is further fueling the demand for CMP machines. Continuous advancements in semiconductor materials and fabrication processes necessitate advanced CMP technologies to meet the requirements of new and emerging applications.</p><p>Furthermore, the trend toward miniaturization and integration of semiconductor devices is creating opportunities for CMP machine manufacturers.
