<p><p>A smart light bulb is an innovative lighting solution that can be controlled remotely through smart devices such as smartphones, tablets, or voice assistants. The increasing adoption of smart home automation systems, rising demand for energy-efficient lighting solutions, and the growing popularity of Internet of Things (IoT) devices and technologies are driving the market's growth. Smart light bulbs use LED technology, which consumes less energy and has a longer lifespan compared to traditional incandescent bulbs. This helps in reducing energy consumption and carbon emissions.</p><p>The smart light bulb market has witnessed a surge in product innovations and technological advancements.
