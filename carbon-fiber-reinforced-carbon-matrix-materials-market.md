<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Carbon Matrix Materials (CFRCM) are advanced composite materials composed of carbon fibers embedded in a carbon matrix. The CFRCM market consists of various types, including Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation Process (LIP). CVD involves the deposition of carbon atoms onto a substrate, forming a solid matrix around the carbon fibers. LIP, on the other hand, immerses carbon fibers in a liquid precursor, which then solidifies to create the carbon matrix. These different processes enable the production of CFRCM with diverse properties and applications.</p></p>
