<p><ul><li>Meshed</li><li>Smooth</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for shelf liners can be categorized into two types: meshed and smooth. Meshed shelf liners are made with a crisscross pattern, allowing for improved airflow and preventing moisture build-up. These liners are ideal for use in pantries, closets, or areas where ventilation is important. On the other hand, smooth shelf liners have a flat surface without any perforations, making them suitable for surfaces that require a stable base, such as kitchen cabinets or drawers. Both types serve the purpose of protecting surfaces, providing grip, and enhancing organization.</p></p>
