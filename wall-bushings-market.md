There are two main types of wall bushings - indoor-indoor and indoor-outdoor. Indoor-indoor wall bushings are designed for use within buildings, connecting power cables between different indoor locations. On the other hand, indoor-outdoor wall bushings are used for connecting power cables that transition between indoor and outdoor environments. Both types of wall bushings play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient electrical connections for various applications.</p></p>
