<p><strong>The Optical splitter Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters (FBT)</li><li>Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters (PLC)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical splitter modules are devices used in fiber optic networks to split or divide an optical signal into multiple paths. Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters (FBT) are traditional splitters that use a fused tapering technique to split the signal. Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters (PLC) are newer technology that employs a waveguide circuit to divide the signal. These splitters offer more precise splitting ratios and exhibit low loss and polarization dependence. Other types of optical splitter modules may include integrated modules or customized designs to cater to specific network requirements, providing flexibility and optimization options.</p></p>
