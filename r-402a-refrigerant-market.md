R-402A offers similar thermodynamic properties to R-502, making it a popular choice for retrofitting existing systems.</p><p>The future outlook for the R-402A refrigerant market appears to be positive. The market is expected to witness significant growth, mainly driven by the phasing out of ozone-depleting refrigerants like R-502. Governments and regulatory bodies globally have imposed strict regulations to reduce the usage of ozone-depleting substances, driving the demand for alternative refrigerants like R-402A.</p><p>Additionally, the increasing demand for cold storage facilities for perishable food items, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals is expected to drive the market growth further.
