<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Loupes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through The Lens Loupe (TTL)</li><li>Flip Up Loupe</li><li>Galilean Loupe</li><li>Prismatic Loupe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Loupe market types can be categorized into four main types. Firstly, the Through The Lens Loupe (TTL) is built into the frames of glasses and provides a wide field of view. Secondly, the Flip Up Loupe is attached to existing glasses and can be flipped up when not in use. Thirdly, the Galilean Loupe is a lightweight option that offers greater magnification but a narrower field of view. Lastly, the Prismatic Loupe uses prisms to widen the field of view without sacrificing magnification.</p></p>
