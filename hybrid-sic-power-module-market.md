The market is driven by the growing demand for electric vehicles (EVs) and hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs), as SiC power modules play a crucial role in these vehicles by improving power conversion efficiency and reducing energy losses. Additionally, the increasing adoption of renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, is driving the demand for SiC power modules in the power generation sector.</p><p>Moreover, the miniaturization trend in consumer electronics and the need for power modules with increased power density and efficiency are also contributing to the market growth.
