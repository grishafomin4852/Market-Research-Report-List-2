<p><ul><li>Engineered</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glued parquet flooring is available in two main types: engineered and solid. Engineered parquet flooring consists of multiple layers of wood veneer stacked together and glued, offering greater stability and resistance to moisture. It is suitable for installation in areas with fluctuating humidity levels. Solid parquet flooring, on the other hand, is made from a single piece of wood and offers a more traditional, authentic look. It is generally recommended for areas with stable moisture levels. Both options provide a stylish and durable flooring solution for residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
