FM radio is commonly used for commercial broadcast stations and is particularly favored for music transmission.</p><p>AM radio, on the other hand, works by varying the amplitude of the carrier wave in relation to the amplitude of the modulating signal. This modulation technique is simpler and allows for a larger coverage area compared to FM radio.
