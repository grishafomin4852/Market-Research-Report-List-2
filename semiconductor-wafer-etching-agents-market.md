<p><p>The Semiconductor Wafer Etching Agents market encompasses two main types: Wet Etching Agents and Dry Etching Agents. Wet Etching involves immersing a semiconductor wafer in a liquid chemical solution to remove excess material and create desired patterns. Wet etching agents are chemical compounds capable of dissolving the specific materials targeted for removal. On the other hand, Dry Etching Agents involve the use of plasma or reactive gases to remove material, providing precise control over etching patterns. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing by enabling the production of intricate circuitry.</p></p>
