<p><strong>The Graphitic Cathode Block Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom Block</li><li>Side Block</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphitic cathode blocks are used in the production of aluminum to provide a conductive surface for the flow of electrical current. There are two types of graphitic cathode blocks: bottom blocks and side blocks. Bottom blocks are placed on the floor of the reduction cell, while side blocks are attached to the walls of the cell. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the stability and integrity of the cell during the aluminum smelting process, ensuring efficient current flow and extending the lifespan of the cathode.</p></p>
