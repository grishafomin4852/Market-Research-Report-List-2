<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The stainless steel cathode plate market includes two types: ladle copper cathode plate and copper clad steel cathode plate. Ladle copper cathode plate is made from stainless steel with a copper layer on top, which enhances its electrical conductivity for better electrolysis processes. On the other hand, copper clad steel cathode plate is a combination of stainless steel and copper, providing a cost-effective alternative to pure copper cathodes. Both types of cathode plates are used in various industries for electro-refining and electrowinning applications.</p></p>
