<p><p>A wall power supply, also known as a wall adapter or wall wart, is a device that converts AC (alternating current) voltage from a wall outlet to DC (direct current) voltage suitable for powering electronic devices. It typically consists of a plug that connects to the wall outlet and a cord that attaches to the device. Wall power supplies are widely used to power various consumer electronics such as smartphones, laptops, tablets, and gaming consoles.</p><p>The market for wall power supplies is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The increasing adoption of electronic devices worldwide and the growing demand for portable and wireless devices are major drivers for market growth. Additionally, the rising need for energy-efficient power supply solutions and the development of smart homes and cities are further fueling the demand for wall power supplies.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements are leading to the introduction of compact and lightweight wall power supplies with improved efficiency. The integration of advanced features such as fast charging, power delivery, and universal compatibility is also contributing to market growth.
