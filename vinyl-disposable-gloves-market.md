<p><p>The vinyl disposable gloves market consists of two main types: powder-free and powder-coated gloves. Powder-free gloves are free from powder substances, making them suitable for individuals with allergies or sensitivities. They are commonly used in industries where contaminants should be minimized, such as food processing or healthcare. On the other hand, powder-coated gloves are coated with a fine powder layer, which allows for easier donning and doffing. This type of glove is often used in industries where a firm grip is required, like automotive or janitorial services.</p></p>
