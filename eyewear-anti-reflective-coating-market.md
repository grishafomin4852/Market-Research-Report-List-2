<p><p>Eyewear anti-reflective coating market can be classified into two types: single layer and multi-layer coatings. Single layer coatings are comprised of a single anti-reflective layer that helps reduce glare and reflection, enhancing visual clarity. On the other hand, multi-layer coatings consist of multiple layers, each with a different refractive index, which increases light transmission and reduces reflection across a wider range of wavelengths. While both types improve visual comfort and cosmetic appeal, multi-layer coatings typically offer better performance and are more durable.</p></p>
