<p><p>Static Torque Sensors are devices used to measure the amount of torque or rotational force being applied in a stationary position. There are two main types of Static Torque Sensors: Rotary Torque Sensors and Reaction Torque Sensors. Rotary Torque Sensors are designed to measure torque in rotating systems, such as motors or engines. On the other hand, Reaction Torque Sensors are used to measure the torque generated by a stationary or fixed object when an external force is applied. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries to ensure accurate torque measurement and control.</p></p>
