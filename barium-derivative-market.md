<p><p>Barium derivatives are chemical compounds that contain the element barium and are derived from it. Barium sulfate, a common barium derivative, is widely used as a contrast agent in X-ray imaging. The growth of the healthcare industry, especially in emerging economies, is expected to fuel the demand for barium derivatives.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising demand for barium-based drilling fluids in the oil & gas industry is also contributing to market growth. Barium derivatives are used as weighting agents in drilling fluids due to their high specific gravity. They help control pressure and prevent blowouts during drilling operations. With the growing exploration and production activities in the oil & gas sector, the demand for barium derivatives is expected to increase.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a shift towards environmentally friendly barium derivatives.
